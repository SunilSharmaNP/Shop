About Kishori Iron Store

15+ Years of Trusted Service to Builders, Contractors, and Homeowners

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Kishori Iron Store began with a simple vision: to provide premium building materials at honest, transparent prices. What started as a modest 5,000 sq ft operation has grown into one of the most trusted hardware suppliers in Dhanushadham and surrounding regions.

Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Sharma, recognized a gap in the market for quality materials without the inflated markups. With years of industry experience and deep connections with certified manufacturers, we established direct relationships to pass the savings directly to our customers.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art 30,000 sq ft facility housing over 50,000 stock units. Our team of experienced consultants works daily to help contractors, builders, and homeowners select the right materials for their projects at competitive prices.

🎯 Our Mission

Provide access to quality building materials at fair prices with exceptional customer service.

🚀 Our Vision

Become the most trusted construction materials supplier in the region by 2030.

✅ Our Commitment

100% genuine ISO & ISI certified products with rigorous quality checks.

🤝 Our Promise

Fair pricing, reliable delivery, and dedicated customer support always.

Our Journey

2009

Foundation Year

Kishori Iron Store is established with 5,000 sq ft facility and focus on quality cement and steel distribution.

2012

Expansion Phase 1

Expanded product range to include plumbing, electrical, and tools. Warehouse expanded to 15,000 sq ft.

2016

Major Milestone

Achieved ISO 9001 certification. Introduced modern inventory management system and customer portal.

2019

Digital Transformation

Launched online ordering system and WhatsApp customer support. Introduced same-day dispatch service.

2022

State-of-the-Art Facility

Relocated to modern 30,000 sq ft climate-controlled warehouse with advanced logistics infrastructure.

2024

Regional Leadership

Recognized as top-rated building materials supplier. Expanded service area to 500km radius.
