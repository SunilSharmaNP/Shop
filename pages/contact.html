Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How can I place a bulk order?

You can contact our sales team via phone, WhatsApp, or email with your requirements. We'll provide you with a customized quote within 24 hours.

❓ What is your delivery range?

We deliver within 500km radius of Dhanushadham. For orders within our coverage area, same-day dispatch is available.

❓ Do you offer credit terms?

Yes, for regular and bulk customers, we offer flexible credit terms. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

❓ Are your products certified?

All our products are ISO 9001 and ISI certified. We source only from certified manufacturers and conduct rigorous quality checks.

❓ What if I have a quality issue?

We have a 100% quality guarantee. Contact our support team immediately with photos/evidence, and we'll resolve it within 48 hours.

❓ Do you provide technical guidance?

Yes, our team of experts can help you select the right materials for your project and provide technical recommendations.

❓ Can I pick up from your warehouse?

Yes, you can arrange to pick up directly from our Dhanushadham warehouse. Please call ahead to confirm availability.

❓ How do I track my order?

We'll provide you with a tracking number via SMS/email. You can also contact our support team for real-time updates.
